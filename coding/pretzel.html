Soft Pretzels

Hours

Difficulty

Yield

Soft pretzels are a yeasted, versatile treat with a chewy interior. Originally from Germany, the soft pretzel can be salty, savory,
and even sweet.

For the dough:

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 3 tablespoons packed light brown sugar
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour (plus more for kneading)
  • 10 tablespoons unsalted butter (plus more for greasing)
  • 1 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1/3 cup baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons coarse salt

For the sauce:

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cider vinegar

Step 1
Warm the milk in the microwave until it reaches about 110F and pour into a medium bowl. Add the yeast, and combine until the yeast falls to the bottom. Allow it to soften until you see it activate: when yeast starts popping up from the bottom of the bowl and appearing bubbly.

Step 2
Stir in the brown sugar and 1 cup of flour with a wooden spoon. Dice 2 tablespoons of butter and soften in the microwave, then stir into the mix. Finally, add the remaining flour and the fine sea salt to make a fairly sticky dough.

Step 3
Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead, adding more flour if needed, until smooth but still slightly tacky (about 5 minutes). Shape into a ball, place in a lightly greased bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Let rise in a warm spot until doubled in size, about 1 hour.

Step 4
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F and grease a large baking sheet. Punch the dough to deflate it, then turn out onto a lightly floured surface. Divide the dough into 6 pieces. Roll and stretch each piece with the palms of your hands into a long rope, holding the ends and slapping the middle of the rope on the counter as you stretch. Form each rope into a pretzel shape: pull the ends up together, twist them around, and then place them down.

Step 5
Dissolve the baking soda in 3 cups warm water in a shallow baking dish. Gently dip each pretzel in the solution, then arrange on the prepared baking sheet and sprinkle with the coarse salt. Bake until golden, 10 to 12 minutes.

Step 6
Prepare the sauce: Combine the mayonnaise, mustard, brown sugar and vinegar in a bowl. Cover and refrigerate.
